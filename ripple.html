Ripple

Project information

  • Category: Bike Safety
  • Project date: 10 June, 2021

With Ripple, we aim to address the communication gap that exists between cyclists, pedesrians and motorists. Ripple is a comprehensive bike safety system which provides haptic feedback to bikers to alert them about approaching hazards, potholes and other cyclists in distress. What makes Ripple different is that it seeks to provide the most utility in the least intrsuvie manner; Ripple works in the background, which allows the users to enjoy their ride with minimal distractions, whilst providing just enough information to make for a safer ride. The Ripple app allows for an easy installation and integrates the utilities offered by this product in an intuitive environment.
